Compose compelling, immersive spatial audio performances and sound artworks, with Spacer.

Spacer is a customizable, personal and intuitive software tool for surround sound composition and performance.

Designed collaboratively with musicians, Spacer empowers sound artists to get up and running with new technolgy and music presentation formats. This project challenges the traditional interface design frameworks for DAWs
(
Digital Audio Workstations
)
that musicians use to compose and produce music. Veering away from standardized and rigid design, Spacer allows for freedom and editing of the user interface itself. Spacer approaches sound spatialization design in an intuitive way, bringing out our childlike wonder and desire for play.
