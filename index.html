COMPUTATIONAL DESIGN
Hello, my name is Zoma, and I'm a designer and educator who uses computer programming as a gateway to interface and experience design, generative graphic arts and tool-making. In my work i'm particularly concerned with novel approaches and cultural understandings interfaces, customizability, ownership and authorship. I investigate and critique the design process at all levels, from labour practices, to design, to deployment and dissemination of tools. I'm currently building a software tools and accompanying UI for music compsition and performance.
